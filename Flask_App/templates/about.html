How are you getting your playlist?

Energy


Energy from Spotify is defined as represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity of a song.

When the user inputs the energy the algorithm finds any song within one point of the energy entered and adds those to a list of possible songs

Happiness


To find happiness I use Valence. Spotify defines valence as the musical positiveness conveyed by a track.

When the user inputs the valence the algorithm finds any song within one point of the valence entered and adds those to a list of possible songs

Song


When the user enters a song that they like the alorithm identifies the songs Topic

The songs topic is found using Topic Modeling which identifies patterns between documents
